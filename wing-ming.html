Wing Ming Farm

Wing Ming (永明) is an Asian, woman-owned flower farm in the Hudson Valley. They grow native perennials and hardy annuals that support pollinators and soil health, while fostering ecological reciprocity for a regenerative future. The work included a logo suite and brand components covering color, type, layout approach, and art direction across digital and physical contexts.

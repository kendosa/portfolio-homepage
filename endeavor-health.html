Brand Identity & Guidelines

Endeavor Health

Following a merger of four Chicago healthcare institutions — Northshore, Swedish Hospital, Northwest Community Healthcare, and Edward-Elmhurst — the new entity needed a unified visual identity. Working with TRIPTK, the team built comprehensive brand guidelines establishing a clear taxonomy for the organisation's complex structure, bespoke logo lockups, a new color palette with approved pairings, a practical typographic hierarchy, and art direction principles.

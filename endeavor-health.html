Brand identity for Endeavor Health, a unified visual system built after the merger of four Chicago healthcare institutions

Following the merger of Northshore, Swedish Hospital, Northwest Community Healthcare, and Edward-Elmhurst, the new entity needed a visual identity capable of holding a complex organisational structure. Working with TRIPTK, the brief was to bring four distinct institutions into a single, coherent brand.

The project produced comprehensive brand guidelines — establishing a clear taxonomy, bespoke logo lockups, a new color palette with approved pairings, a practical typographic hierarchy, and art direction principles to govern the system going forward.

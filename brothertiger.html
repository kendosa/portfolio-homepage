Out of Touch — 10th Anniversary Edition

A decade on from its original release, Out of Touch returned as an ultra-limited double vinyl reissue — lagoon-colored wax, die-cut gatefold jacket, and four sides of material spanning the original album, unreleased demos, B-sides, and live recordings. The packaging was designed to carry that weight, pairing a painted cover by John Jagos Sr. with a layout that treated the anniversary as an event rather than a reprint.

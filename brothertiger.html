Brothertiger

An ongoing collaboration

Brothertiger is an electronic musician based in New York. I've been his designer since 2012. This page collects the packaging, art direction, and visual work built across fourteen years of releases.

Promotional motion asset developed for the record label's release campaign for Out Of Touch's 10 year anniversary re-release. The detail of this package was best illustrated in motion, creating a physical presence suited for social.

I designed this logo mark for Brothertiger, a tiger-stripe motif built into a circular form that reads clearly at any size. It now shows up everywhere from vinyl labels and merch to social and motion, a consistent thread across print and digital.

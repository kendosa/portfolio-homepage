Cardinal Renewables

Case Study

Building a leading renewable energy platform to accelerate the energy transition.

Cardinal Renewables is an independent power producer focused on developing, constructing, and operating utility-scale renewable energy projects. Through Carlyle's Renewable and Sustainable Energy Fund, we are supporting the rapid scaling of clean energy generation across the United States, reducing carbon emissions at portfolio-wide scale.

Our investment thesis is predicated on both the compelling risk-adjusted returns of renewable energy and the opportunity to generate measurable, positive environmental impact — demonstrating that climate resilience and investment performance are mutually reinforcing.

Accolade Wines

Case Study

"To paraphrase an old saying, if you show me a well-managed sustainability program, I'll show you a well-managed company."

Accolade Wines not only holds their UK business as one of the UK's top certified Manufacturers of the Year in 2019, but also the Sustainable Manufacturer from the Manufacturer's Association. Their goal of targeting net zero carbon emissions by 2030 is already well underway.

In 2020, Accolade reduced CO₂ emissions at their bottling facility by 44% — from 3,345 to 1,873 tonnes — ahead of schedule, through investment in renewable infrastructure and operational efficiency programs.

Stronger Communities

Accelerate Learning

Case Study

Democratizing access to high-quality science education across underserved communities.

Accelerate Learning provides K–12 science curriculum solutions that meet students where they are — with adaptive, inquiry-based programs used in over 5,000 schools across the United States. The company's products are specifically designed to serve Title I schools, making rigorous STEM education accessible to all students regardless of socioeconomic background.

Since Carlyle's acquisition, Accelerate has expanded its reach by 38%, with measurable improvements in student outcomes in schools serving predominantly low-income communities.

Sustainable Growth

Jeanologia

Case Study

Reducing water and chemical usage in denim processes to transform a global industry.

Jeanologia's technologies are used to create roughly 15% of the 6 billion jeans produced each year — with dramatically reduced water, energy, and chemical consumption compared to conventional processes. An ESG-linked loan signed in 2019 tied the company's financing directly to its sustainability performance, creating direct financial incentives for environmental progress.

The ESG-linked loan structure was a first for Carlyle and demonstrates our commitment to innovative financing mechanisms that align capital with impact.

Diverse Teams

HireVue

Case Study

Diversity is a central priority for many companies — HireVue's core business model helps solve these challenges.

HireVue is supporting Fortune 500 companies to continue to recruit candidates during the pandemic across a wide variety of entry-level and experienced roles. Its support of companies who rely on university recruiting is helping reshape the direct to graduate hiring landscape. Additionally, HireVue is offering free on-demand access to hospitals across the nation during this crisis.

In October 2019, Carlyle Partners VII partnered with HireVue, a tech company which provides virtual interviewing and assessment technologies to help companies find the most qualified and diverse candidates more quickly.

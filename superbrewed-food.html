Brand identity for Superbrewed Food, a biotech company producing post-fermentation protein

Superbrewed Food is a biotech company producing post-fermentation protein — a category that needed a visual language capable of communicating both scientific credibility and consumer approachability at the same time.

The work established an identity that could scale across packaging, digital, and communications — grounded enough for a science-forward audience and accessible enough for the broader consumer market.

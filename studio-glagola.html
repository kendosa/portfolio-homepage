Brand identity for Studio Glagola, a custom furniture and cabinetry shop in the Hudson Valley

Studio Glagola is a custom furniture and cabinetry shop in the Hudson Valley working across trade and consumer contexts — from trade presentations to local markets. The brief was for an identity capable of shifting register without losing coherence.

The work produced a utilitarian logo suite and a dynamic, flexible type and image system — built to function equally well on a business card, a trade sheet, or a market stall banner.

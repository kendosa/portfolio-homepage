Studio Glagola

Brand identity for Studio Glagola, a custom furniture and cabinetry shop in the Hudson Valley

Studio Glagola makes custom furniture and cabinetry in the Hudson Valley. The work is precise, considered, and built to outlast trends. The brand needed to be the same.

I built a utilitarian logo suite around Neue Haas Grotesk and a monogram mark, then developed a type and image system flexible enough to show up seriously on a business card and loosely across social. Restraint at the core, range at the edges.

The work covers logo design, full brand identity, type system, and art direction across photography and layout.

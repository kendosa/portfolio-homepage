Visual identity and design system for Piermont Bank, a digitally-native commercial bank

Piermont Bank is a digitally-native commercial bank built for modern businesses. The brief was to develop a visual identity and design system for a new kind of financial institution — one that needed to project trust without defaulting to convention.

The work balanced approachability and authority across brand and digital touchpoints, establishing a system capable of growing with the bank as it scales.
